Academic Programs

Shaping Futures at Every Stage

Our curriculum is designed to provide age-appropriate, engaging, and rigorous education at every level

🎨 Pre-Primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Our pre-primary program lays a strong foundation through play-based and activity-oriented learning. We create a warm, caring environment where young learners explore, discover, and develop essential skills.

  • Activity-based & experiential learning
  • Phonics & early literacy development
  • Number concepts & basic reasoning
  • Creative arts, music, and story time
  • Motor skill development through play
  • Safe and nurturing classrooms

📚 Primary School (Class I - V)

The primary school program builds strong academic foundations while encouraging curiosity and creativity. Students learn through a balanced mix of classroom instruction, hands-on activities, and group projects.

  • English, Hindi, Punjabi, Mathematics
  • Environmental Science (EVS)
  • Computer Education from Class I
  • Art, Craft & Music classes
  • Physical Education & Games
  • Regular assessments & parent-teacher meetings

🔬 Middle School (Class VI - VIII)

Middle school is where students begin to explore subjects in greater depth. We emphasize analytical thinking, scientific temper, and the development of strong study habits that will serve them throughout life.

  • Science, Mathematics, Social Studies
  • English, Hindi, Punjabi languages
  • Computer Science & IT skills
  • Laboratory-based science learning
  • Co-curricular activities & clubs
  • Personality development workshops

⚛️ Secondary School (Class IX - X)

Our secondary school program focuses on thorough board exam preparation while maintaining a holistic approach to education. Expert faculty provides targeted guidance to help students achieve excellent results.

  • Board-oriented curriculum with in-depth coverage
  • Regular mock tests & performance tracking
  • Science, Mathematics, Social Science, Languages
  • Practical lab sessions for Science
  • Career guidance and counselling
  • Extra classes for weak areas

🎓 Senior Secondary (Class XI - XII)

At the senior secondary level, students choose from specialized streams — Science, Commerce, or Arts — with focused preparation for board exams and competitive entrance tests.

  • Science Stream: Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics
  • Commerce Stream: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics
  • Arts Stream: History, Political Science, Sociology
  • Competitive exam preparation (JEE, NEET, etc.)
  • Career counselling & college guidance
  • Practical labs & project work
