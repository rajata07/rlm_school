Our History & Legacy

RLM Public Sr. Sec. School was established in 1996 with a vision to provide quality education to students in Kapurthala and surrounding areas. What started as a humble beginning has now blossomed into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Over the past three decades, we have maintained an unwavering commitment to academic excellence, character building, and holistic development. Our school offers education from Pre-Primary to Senior Secondary level, catering to students across all age groups.

Under the dynamic leadership of our Director, Shri K.K. Arora, the school has continuously evolved to meet the changing demands of education while staying rooted in Indian values and traditions.

Our Essence

Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Our Vision

To be a centre of educational excellence that inspires students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and compassionate leaders. We envision a school where every child discovers their unique potential and is empowered to make a positive difference in the world.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, safe, and stimulating learning environment that promotes academic excellence, moral values, and all-round personality development. We are committed to equipping our students with 21st-century skills while instilling a deep respect for Indian culture and heritage.
