What We Offer

Modern amenities and resources to support every aspect of your child's education

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled classrooms with digital smart boards and audio-visual teaching aids for interactive learning experiences.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for hands-on experiments and practical learning sessions.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with latest hardware and software, providing students with essential digital literacy and coding skills.

Library

A well-stocked library with thousands of books, reference materials, newspapers, and magazines to fuel the reading habit.

Sports Grounds

Spacious playgrounds for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics to promote physical fitness and sportsmanship.

Transport Facility

Safe and reliable bus service covering all major routes in and around Kapurthala with GPS tracking for parents' peace of mind.

Music & Dance Room

Dedicated space for performing arts with musical instruments and a dance floor to nurture artistic talents.

Art & Craft Room

Fully equipped art studio where students explore their creativity through drawing, painting, sculpture, and various crafts.

Medical Room

On-campus medical facility with first-aid and basic healthcare provisions. Regular health check-ups for all students.

Activity Hall

Multipurpose hall for assemblies, cultural programs, celebrations, parent-teacher meetings, and indoor events.

Clean Drinking Water

RO purified drinking water stations available across the campus ensuring safe and hygienic drinking water for all.

CCTV Surveillance

Complete campus coverage with CCTV cameras ensuring safety and security of all students and staff members.
