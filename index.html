Welcome to RLM Public Sr. Sec. School

Established in 1996, RLM Public Sr. Sec. School has been a beacon of quality education in Kapurthala for over three decades. Under the visionary leadership of our Director, Shri K.K. Arora, the school has grown into a premier educational institution that nurtures holistic development.

We provide comprehensive education from Pre-Primary to Senior Secondary level, focusing on academic excellence, moral values, and all-round personality development of every child.

Our Programs

Academic Programs We Offer

Comprehensive education pathways designed for every stage of your child's growth

Pre-Primary

A warm and playful environment for our youngest learners (Nursery, LKG, UKG) with activity-based learning.

Primary (I - V)

Building strong foundations in academics, language, and creativity through engaging teaching methods.

Middle School (VI - VIII)

Fostering curiosity and critical thinking with hands-on labs, projects, and co-curricular activities.

Secondary (IX - X)

Board-focused preparation with expert guidance, regular assessments, and comprehensive study material.

Sr. Secondary (XI - XII)

Specialized streams in Science, Commerce, and Arts with career counseling and competitive exam preparation.
